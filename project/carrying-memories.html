Carrying Memories

The Digital Craftsmanship course's objective was to create a data-driven bag that held something. Carrying Memories is a deeply personal bag that holds memories of a loved one who has past. It is a bag you create yourself or with those who knew the person. The bag stores, carries, and shares those cherished memories to remember that person. The bag is meant to shrink and eventually be put away in tandem with the grieving process, however that might occur.

This project involved deep exploration into parametric design, materials, the complex way grief intertwines in interpersonal relationships, data visualization, and layering in design. Each person in the group with their skills, personalities, and history made this bag a reality. This project formed a big part of my ethos as a designer.
