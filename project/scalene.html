Scalene

A team project. The culmination of all our prototypes and concepts relating to the sun, light, sound, and tangibility. When the sun shines, the two layers of coloured triangles mix and shine their colours on the surfaces of the design space. Each triangle has its unique sound dependent on the colour. The size and shape determine its presence and pitch. Each design student can add to the soundscape that will play every 10 degrees of the sun’s rotation. The responsiveness of dropping triangles and how they fall imperfectly creates suprise in dynamics and expression. Hovering tiles over the lip previews the triangle’s unique sound, giving the user feedforward in their action Triangles were chosen for their simple shape yet varying angles, allowing unique patterns to emerge in the frame.
